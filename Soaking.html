The Van’s Warm Embrace

Heidi slips into Ali’s van after work, drawn by his text and the promise of escape. The heat of the van wraps around her like a lover’s embrace, the scent of leather and bergamot mingling with the metallic tang of his tools.

The workplace lights flickered overhead like dying fireflies, casting long shadows across the empty desks as she slipped her phone back into her purse.

The back street smelled of damp pavement and diesel fumes, the air thick with the kind of chill that seeped through wool coats as she searched for Ali's van waiting in the dim light.

Forever Yours, Ali
